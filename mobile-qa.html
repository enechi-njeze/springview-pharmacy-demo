Springview Mobile QA Harness

Loads the real site at phone widths and runs automated layout checks against the true rendered pixels. Open this from the deployed site (e.g. your-site.netlify.app/mobile-qa.html) or locally next to index.html.

Manual eyeball pass (do these by hand on the 390px frame):
  • Language gate fills the screen; the 4 language buttons are each easily tappable.
  • Skip the intro; header is sticky; the quick-action pills scroll sideways without breaking the layout.
  • Bottom-right: WhatsApp (green) sits above Galaxy (cosmic). They do not overlap.
  • Open Galaxy: the panel fills nearly the width with margins; WhatsApp bubble disappears while it is open.
  • Start the tour (bottom-left button): each card is fully readable, never covers the highlighted item, and the progress bar advances.
  • Shop: filter controls sit at the top; product grid is 1-2 columns; tapping a card opens the product page.
  • Rotate through EN / ES / HT / FR: nothing clips or overflows; longer French/Creole strings still fit.
